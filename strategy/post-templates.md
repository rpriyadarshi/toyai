## Template Customization Tips

1. **Keep first 3 lines as hook** - These are visible without clicking
2. **Use line breaks** - Make posts scannable
3. **Include visuals** - Photos, code snippets, diagrams
4. **Ask questions** - Drive engagement in comments
5. **Link to resources** - Make it easy for people to learn more
6. **Use hashtags strategically** - 5-7 hashtags, mix of broad and niche
7. **Respond to comments** - Especially in first hour after posting

---

## Post Length Guidelines

- **Short posts**: 150-300 words (quick insights)
- **Medium posts**: 300-600 words (standard technical posts)
- **Long posts**: 600-1000 words (deep dives, reflections)

Mix post lengths for variety. LinkedIn favors engagement over length.

---

## Visual Elements to Include

1. **Hand calculation photos** - Show your work
2. **Code snippets** - Formatted nicely
3. **Diagrams** - From book/images/
4. **Before/after comparisons** - Show progress
5. **Screenshots** - Of code output, terminal, etc.

Visuals increase engagement significantly.
